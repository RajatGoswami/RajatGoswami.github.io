Résumé

Connect5

This was an independently developed iOS, single player strategy board game. CPU opponent is implemented using the minimax algorithm. Players take turns placing tiles on gameboard until a horizontal, vertical or diagonal connection of their game pieces is made.

StoreTracker iOS & Web App

Hack the North submission created in collaboration with 3 other members. The iOS app recognizes and collects distance data from Estimote Beacons. This data is then uploaded to the web app to be graphed for visual analysis. Its purpose is to provide retailers with information on which of their in-store products attract the most attention.

CC3k

ChamberCrawler3000 is a simplified Rogue-like game developed in collaboration with another student for my 2nd year Object-Oriented Programming course. The implementation is completely in C++ and includes random player, enemy and item generation, player and enemy movement, potions, treasure, combat, and 8-level gameplay.
