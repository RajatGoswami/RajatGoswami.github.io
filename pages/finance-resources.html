Financial Learning Resources

Investing Master Class by WealthSimple

WealthSimple is the investment management firm trying to become "the world's most human financial company". It strives to do so by making financial services more accessible to younger people and has many learning resources on their site oriented towards novice investors.

Their "Investing Master Class" features a series of videos that answer common questions from beginners with a sense of humour. It does feature quite a bit of self-promotion, but I would recommend watching the following lessons:

  • Lesson 4: The Five Simple Rules for Investing

  • Lesson 5: The Waterfall Method

Lesson 4 provides advice that is relevant regardless of age or investing experience while lesson 5 features a "Financial Fitness Check" to help determine the steps you need to take before you can comfortably invest your money.
